Zebras Recipe

time:

  • preparation time: 30 min
  • baking time: 30 min

ingredients for cake:

  • cake flour: 170g
  • butter: 150g
  • sour cream 18%: 200g
  • sugar: 150g
  • eggs: 4 medium
  • cocoa: 2 teaspoons
  • baking soda: 2 teaspoons

recipe for cake:

  1. We need to take off eggs, butter and sour cream from fridge earlier.
  2. Add eggs to the bowl, later sugar and soft butter. Blend everything with mixer few minutes. Reduce rotation and slowly add sour cream and later flour and baking soda.
  3. In next bowl you add cocoa and half of blended cake. Mix everything once more a few minutes.
  4. Now you need to add everything to form. Lets use cake tin. Add everything in turns, some of white mass, and some of cocoa, etc.
  5. We need to bake with top/down function with 170°C. After 30 min we need to check on cake.
